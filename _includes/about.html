About

Tucked within an apple orchard on Martha’s Vineyard, Henry’s Animation Studio is an independent stop motion film space dedicated to handcrafted storytelling. Here, miniature sets, sculpted figures, and frame-by-frame precision bring surreal, tactile narratives to life in an environment shaped by nature and artistic intent.

The studio doubles as a creative workshop—part animation lab, part sculptural playground—where materials are reimagined and techniques evolve with each project. Henry’s work emphasizes process, patience, and the physicality of the medium, offering a deeply personal alternative to digital animation’s polished surfaces.
